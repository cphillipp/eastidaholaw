Dennis was raised in Idaho Falls and is very pleased to have the opportunity to practice law in this community. Dennis graduated from Idaho State University in 1995 with Bachelor degrees in Political Science and Spanish. He received his Juris Doctorate degree from the University of Idaho College of Law in 1999. After graduating from law school Dennis worked for the Bannock County Prosecuting Attorney's Office in Pocatello as a deputy prosecutor becoming the chief deputy prosecutor in 2003. In 2006, Dennis and his family moved to the Treasure Valley to pursue a civil litigation practice.

In May of 2009, Dennis joined Thompson, Smith, Woolf & Anderson. He and his family love the outdoors and the many recreational opportunities available in Eastern Idaho. His primary areas of practice are focused on personal injury, wrongful death and general civil litigation.
